Warm Colors

Red

Red is a color that evokes strong emotions, often associated with passion, love and energy. It can also signify danger or urgency.

Orange

Orange is a vibrant color that combines the energy of red and the happiness of yellow. It is often associated with enthusiasm, creativity, and warmth.

Yellow

Yellow is a bright and cheerful color that symbolizes happiness, optimism, and sunshine. It can also represent caution or cowardice in some contexts.

Cool Colors

Green

Green is a color that represents nature, growth, and renewal. It is often associated with tranquility and health.

Blue

Blue is a calming color that symbolizes trust, loyalty, and wisdom. It can also evoke feelings of sadness or melancholy.

Purple

Purple is a color that combines the stability of blue and the energy of red. It is often associated with royalty, luxury, and spirituality.
